Get ready to code!

The Perth Code Dojo is open to developers of any language who want to better themselves and improve their programming skills. The groups main focus is around software craftsmanship and sharing with others. This Dojo is primarily aimed at the professional community but newbies to programming are also welcome to attend.

At each Dojo a new problem to solve will be presented, participants then pair up and are encouraged to use test driven development and other XP (Extreme Programming) techniques to solve the challenge. At the end the pairs are invited to share their approach. This proves to be a great way to improve as a programmer and to learn in and safe and fun environment.
